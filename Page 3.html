MY FAVOURITE PLAYERS

Of all the players in every league my two favorites are Cristiano Ronaldo and Angel Di Maria.

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro, commonly known as Cristiano Ronaldo, is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Italian club Juventus and captains the Portugal national team. Before joining Juventus, he played for the clubs, Manchester United and Real Madrid. Ronaldo has a record-tying five Ballon d'Or awards, the most for a European player, and is the first player to win four European Golden Shoes. He has won 27 trophies in his career, including five league titles, five UEFA Champions League titles and one UEFA European Championship. He is often considered the best player in the world and regarded by many as one of the greatest players of all time.

Angel Fabian Di Maria is an Argentine professional footballer who plays for the french football club, Paris Saint-Germain, and the Argentina national team. He was formerly a frequent starting 11 player for the Manchester United, FC Barcelona and Real Madrid Football Clubs. He is well known for his skills. He can play as either a winger or attacking midfielder.
